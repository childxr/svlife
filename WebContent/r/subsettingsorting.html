Subseting & Ordering Data Frame in R

Subsetting

  var1 var2 var3
1 2 NA 15
4 1 10 11
2 3 NA 12
3 5 6 14
5 4 9 13

Subseting can be done by using index or name (可以用索引或行列名取到某行或某列)

  • X[,1] or X[,"var1"] => [1] 2 1 3 5 4

Get concecutive rows/cols by seq (可以用序列取到连续的行列)

  • X[1:2, "var2"] => [1] NA 10

Get rows by conditions (可以用条件取行)

  • X[(X$var1 <= 3 & X$var3 > 11), ] => no. 1, 2 get extracted

Get rid of NA by which command (可以用which命令处理NA)

  • X[which(X$var2 > 8),] => no. 4, 5 get extracted

Ordering

ordering by a specific columns (根据某一列来排序)

  • X[order(X$var1),]

ordering by two columns (根据某两列排序)

  • X[order(X$var2, X$var1)]

ordering with package plyr (用plyr包排序)

  • arrange(X, var1)
  • arrange(X, desc(var1))
